Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 16, 2024

At KarakusApp, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our applications ("App"). This Privacy Policy is part of our Terms of Use.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account or use the App, we may collect your email address and other registration details
  • Input Content: Text prompts, photos, and videos you upload to use App features
  • User Content: All content generated through the App
  • Communication Data: When you contact us for support, we collect your email and message content

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information
  • Usage Data: Features you use, time spent on the App, interaction patterns, performance data
  • App Activity: Crash reports, error logs, and diagnostic information

1.3 Payment and Subscription Information

Payment processing is handled securely by Apple App Store. We do not store your credit card or payment information. We receive transaction confirmations and subscription status from the App Store.

2. Photo Data & AI Processing

2.1 How We Process Your Photos

When you upload photos to the App for AI transformation:

  • Photos are securely transmitted to our processing servers via encrypted connections
  • Photos are processed by our AI models to generate transformations
  • Automatic Deletion: All uploaded photos are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers within 24 hours of upload
  • Generated content remains accessible to you until you delete it or terminate your account

2.2 Face Data & Image Processing

IMPORTANT: We do not collect, analyze, or store facial data or biometric identifiers.

All photos, including those that may contain faces, are processed solely to apply visual effects and AI-generated transformations. We do NOT:

  • Perform facial recognition or face mapping
  • Create, infer, or retain biometric data
  • Identify individuals from facial features
  • Match faces across different photos
  • Share face data with third parties for identification purposes

2.3 Data Security During Processing

  • All photos are transmitted using industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL)
  • Photos are stored temporarily in secure, access-controlled storage during processing
  • We implement strict access controls to prevent unauthorized access
  • No Sharing: We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your uploaded photos with third parties
  • No Retention: Uploaded photos are never used to train AI models

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

  • To provide and deliver the App's services
  • To process your Input Content and generate Output Content
  • To manage your account, subscriptions, and in-app purchases
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries

3.2 Service Improvement

  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the App's features
  • To monitor and analyze App performance, identify technical issues, and fix bugs
  • To develop new features and services

4. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

We use trusted third-party services to operate and improve the App:

4.1 AI Processing

We use AI processing services for transformations. These services operate under strict data processing agreements and do not retain uploaded images after processing.

4.2 Payment Processing

Apple App Store handles all payment processing, subscription management, and transaction handling. We do not have access to your payment card information.

4.3 Analytics and Performance

  • Firebase Analytics: App usage statistics and performance monitoring
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reporting and error tracking

4.4 Data Sharing Limitations

We do not sell your personal information. We only share your information:

  • With the third-party service providers listed above, solely for the purposes described
  • When required by law, legal process, or government request
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users

5. Data Retention

  • Uploaded Photos: Automatically deleted within 24 hours of upload
  • Generated Content: Accessible until you manually delete it or terminate your account
  • Account Data: Retained for as long as your account is active

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • Access: You can access your personal information through your account settings
  • Correction: You can update or correct your account information at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete your generated content or entire account
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data

6.2 Your Legal Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights:

  • Right to Know what personal data we collect
  • Right to Delete your personal information
  • Right to Object to certain processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us at karakusapp@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted is encrypted using TLS/SSL protocols
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on secure servers with encryption at rest
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security assessments

9. International Data Transfers

The App's services and servers may be located in various countries. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: karakusapp@gmail.com

By using KarakusApp, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Privacy Policy.

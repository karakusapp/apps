Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 16, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how LocaleX ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

üîí Privacy First: LocaleX is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your translation content and API keys never touch our servers - everything stays on your device or goes directly to your chosen AI provider.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

LocaleX does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your translation content: Your .xcstrings files, .strings files, and all translation text remain on your device
  • Your API keys: API keys are stored locally on your device using secure storage (Keychain)
  • Your project files: All Xcode project data stays on your Mac
  • Personal identification information: We don't collect names, emails, or addresses through the App
  • Usage tracking data: We don't track how you use the App

3. Information Stored Locally

The following information is stored locally on your device:

  • API Keys: Stored securely in macOS Keychain
  • App Preferences: Your settings and preferences
  • Translation Memory: Previously translated strings for consistency
  • Custom Glossary: Your defined terms and translations
  • Recent Projects: Paths to recently opened projects

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

4. Third-Party AI Services

4.1 How API Communication Works

When you use AI translation features, your translation content is sent directly from your device to your chosen AI provider:

  • OpenAI (ChatGPT)
  • Anthropic (Claude)
  • Google (Gemini)

This communication happens directly between your device and the AI provider's servers. LocaleX acts only as a client and does not intercept, store, or process this data on any intermediate servers.

4.2 Third-Party Privacy Policies

Your use of AI translation services is subject to the privacy policies of your chosen provider:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

We recommend reviewing these policies to understand how your translation content may be used by these services.

4.3 API Key Security

Your API keys are:

  • Stored locally in macOS Keychain (encrypted)
  • Never transmitted to LocaleX servers (we have no servers)
  • Used only to authenticate directly with your chosen AI provider
  • Under your complete control - you can revoke them at any time

5. App Store Data

When you purchase or download LocaleX through the App Store, Apple may collect certain information according to their privacy policies. This is handled by Apple, not by LocaleX. Please review:

  • Apple Privacy Policy

6. Analytics and Crash Reporting

LocaleX does not include any analytics SDKs or crash reporting services. We do not collect:

  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports
  • Performance metrics
  • Device information

7. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect locally stored data:

  • Keychain Storage: Sensitive data like API keys are stored in macOS Keychain
  • No Network Transmission: Your data is not sent to our servers because we don't have any
  • Direct API Communication: All AI service communication uses HTTPS encryption

8. Children's Privacy

LocaleX is a professional development tool and is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: All data is stored on your device and accessible to you
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data
  • API Keys: You can delete or change your API keys at any time in App settings
  • Translation Memory: You can clear translation memory from within the App

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
